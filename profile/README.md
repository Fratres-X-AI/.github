# Fratres-X-AI

**Defense Technology Prototypes & Systems Architecture**

We design high-impact prototype systems at the intersection of advanced sensors, kinetic effects, hypersonics, AI-driven decision systems, and autonomous operations.

### Approach

The modern battlespace is evolving rapidly. We continuously expand our skill sets and technical toolkit to address emerging threats — from hypersonic weapons and drone swarms to contested electromagnetic environments and AI-enabled adversarial systems.

Our work is grounded in **physics-first modeling**, honest uncertainty quantification, and rapid iteration from concept through working prototypes.

### Current Focus Areas
- Hypersonic tracking and reachable-set prediction in degraded environments
- Kinetic and effects-based counter-drone / swarm defense
- Multi-domain sensor fusion and decision support
- AI-augmented systems architecture for contested domains

### Active Prototypes

- **[RADR-mk.60](https://github.com/Fratres-X-AI/RADR-mk.60)** — Recoilless Anti-Drone Rocket System
- **[MFKS-Kinetic-Cloud](https://github.com/Fratres-X-AI/MFKS-Kinetic-Cloud)** — Terminal Kinetic Cloud Defense
- **[MS-V](https://github.com/Fratres-X-AI/MS-V)** — Multi-Spectral Obscurant Grenade
- **[SHEAR](https://github.com/Fratres-X-AI/SHEAR)** — Sensor-Degraded Hypersonic Engagement & Reachable Set Prediction

### Philosophy

We prioritize **adaptability**, technical honesty, and building systems that remain relevant as threats and technology evolve. All public repositories reflect work at its current maturity level.
